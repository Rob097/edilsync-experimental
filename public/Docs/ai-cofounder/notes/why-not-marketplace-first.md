**Why Not Marketplace-First**
Leading with a marketplace would recreate the fatal flaws already validated in research: contractors hate paying $50-100+ per lead with no conversion guarantee, leads sold to 5+ contractors create price-shopping races, and platforms abandon users after connection—right when the real pain begins. Coordination platform first solves **immediate daily pain**, creates sticky adoption through project history, and makes the marketplace a natural extension later: "I want my next project to run this smoothly too, so I'll hire someone already on the platform."
